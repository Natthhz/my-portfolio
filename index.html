About Me

I'm a Web & Mobile Developer

I specialize in turning ideas into real, functional applications that people enjoy using, whether it's on the web or mobile. With a strong foundation in technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Flutter, I focus on building user-friendly interfaces and smooth experiences across devices.
